About Me

I am a senior at Indiana University Bloomington, pursuing a computer science degree with a specialization in cybersecurity. I am set to graduate in May 2025. Born and raised in Park Ridge, Illinois, playing sports was a significant part of my childhood, and it remains an important part of my life. I played basketball and baseball throughout my youth and currently play slow-pitch softball. I also have a deep love for movies and TV, being a huge Game of Thrones fan, with "Get Out" as my favorite movie. Experiencing both of these masterpieces for the first time is something I wish I could relive. I began coding in my junior year of high school and haven't stopped since. The satisfaction of completing a project or solving a problem is something I cherish. Throughout my coding journey, I have worked with Java, Python, and C, and I hope to learn many more languages. I have devoted most of my time to studying data structures, computer systems, and object-oriented programming. Studying computer science has opened my eyes to the endless possibilities this field offers, and I hope to learn as much as I can about computer science and create something that helps people across the world.
